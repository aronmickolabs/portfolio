About Me

I run Aron Micko Labs, a one-person studio designing, building, and publishing Android apps end to end, while working full time as a Creative Writer and Junior Programmer. My faith, my technical training, and my habit of drawing and writing all end up in the same body of work.

01
Full-stack solo developer

One person, every part of the pipeline

From product idea to Flutter and Android Studio build, to Play Console submission and marketing copy, I run the entire lifecycle of an app myself under the Aron Micko Labs brand.

02
Design shaped by faith

Wellness tools with Scripture built in

Several of my apps carry Catholic faith features by design: prayer journals, Scripture-based calm modes, and CBT-informed reflection tools sit next to the technical work.

03
A writer first

Six published novels, one adapted into a game

Before I wrote code professionally, I was writing fiction. That instinct for narrative shows up in how I design onboarding flows, empty states, and in-app copy today.
